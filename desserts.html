Mango Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 packet unflavored gelatin
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a small bowl, sprinkle the gelatin over the water and let it sit for 5 minutes to bloom.
  2. In a blender, combine the chopped mangoes, sugar, coconut milk, and heavy cream. Blend until smooth.
  3. Heat the gelatin mixture in the microwave for about 15-20 seconds until it dissolves completely.
  4. Add the dissolved gelatin to the mango mixture and blend again to combine.
  5. Pour the mixture into individual serving cups or bowls.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.
  7. Garnish with fresh mint leaves before serving, if desired.

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Melt the dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl over a pot of simmering water. Stir until smooth and let it cool slightly.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks and sugar together until pale and creamy.
  3. Gradually add the melted chocolate to the egg mixture, stirring constantly.
  4. In another bowl, whip the heavy cream, vanilla extract, and salt until soft peaks form.
  5. Gently fold the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture until fully combined.
  6. In a clean bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  7. Fold the egg whites into the chocolate mixture in three additions.
  8. Divide the mousse into serving dishes and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
