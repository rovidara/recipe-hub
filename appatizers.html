Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) button mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Clean mushrooms and remove stems, setting caps aside.
  3. Finely chop the mushroom stems.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and chopped mushroom stems. Cook until soft.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in breadcrumbs, Parmesan, cream cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  6. Stuff each mushroom cap with the mixture and place on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes or until tops are golden.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!

Caprese Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 20 cherry tomatoes
  • 20 small fresh mozzarella balls (bocconcini)
  • 2 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 20 small skewers or toothpicks

Steps:

  1. On each skewer, thread one cherry tomato, one basil leaf, and one mozzarella ball.
  2. Arrange the skewers on a serving platter.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  4. Sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.
